CBRE Occupier Longlist Creator

Please read carefully: This tool allows you to create your longlist slides automatically, it works in the following way:

  • First, download the template, this is where you can populate your data, every row is a new property option
  • In the template, the columns image 1 and image 2 are the images you will place on the corresponding property slide, the images you upload need to have the same name as mentioned in the excel, in the case of the excel template the images would be called 1, 2, 3, 4
  • After you have filled the template with data and have all the pictures named corresponding to the names in your data file you can upload your excel and pictures below
  • Then click on Generate Powerpoint and your longlist will automatically be created
